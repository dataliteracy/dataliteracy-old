Participant Projects

As part of the first phase of the Nepal Data Literacy Program, participants worked on data driven projects in key sector areas across the SDG Goals. These projects were presented to a panel of Judges, who chose the winners in accordance to preselected criteria. Some of the judging criteria included - projects having a clear audience, meeting a need, using available data and involving relevant data analysis.

See below details of each group project.



Winning Teams and Projects


Team 15

Access to Finance for Women of Nepal

Our project focus on current barriers that is plaguing women's access to finance and propose potential solution to mitigate it.

Team 5

Analysis of Budget Allocation and Spending of the Provinces in Nepal

We are trying to analyze the budget allocation and spending of the provinces and check are they aligned with the SDGs.

Team 1

Education for People with Disability - Are We Doing Enough?

An analysis of the existing gaps in the government policies for inclusive education for people with disabilities.

Team-2 Pawan K Jha, Madhu Sudan Gautam, Ojeshwi Risal, Bhim Gautam Assessing the livelihood recovery after the 2015 Nepal earthquake in most affected districts The proposed project assesses the livelihood recovery after the 2015 Nepal earthquake on different fronts (houses reconstruction, community infrastructures, services, goods, cultures, traditions, etc). It is specifically related with taxis that runs in Kathmandu valley.
